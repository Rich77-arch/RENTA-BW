About

RENTA is a locally developed, professional platform designed for the housing needs of individuals in Botswana. It serves both tenants and landlords by facilitating the house-hunting process for renters and enabling landlords or real estate agencies to advertise available properties. The platform is user-friendly and cost-effective, making it accessible to minimum wage workers and students. Additionally, RENTA caters to the preferences of affluent individuals seeking high-end real estate options. In essence, RENTA offers a comprehensive solution for diverse housing requirements in Botswana. .
